MANIPULATIVE THERAPY

Manipulative therapy, is a physical treatment to treat musculoskeletal pain and disability; it most includes kneading and manipulation of muscles, joint mobilization and joint manipulation.

CUPPING

Cupping therapy is an ancient form of alternative medicine in which a therapist puts special cups on your skin for a few minutes to create suction, which helps with pain, inflammation, blood flow, relaxation and well-being.

HYDROTHERAPY

Hydrotherapy is the use of water in the treatment of different conditions, including arthritis and related rheumatic complaints. It involves special exercises that you do in a warm-water pool.

CRYOTHERAPY

Cryotherapy is the local or general use of low temperatures in medical therapy. Cryotherapy may be used to treat a variety of tissue lesions.

STRENGHENING EXERCICES

Muscle and bone strenghening exercices such as stretching, chin tuck and many more.

INFRARED

Infrared therapy is a simple and painless therapy that uses light to treat acute or chronic pain.
