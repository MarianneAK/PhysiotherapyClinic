FEATURED TECHNIQUES

MANIPULATIVE THERAPY

MANIPULATIVE THERAPY

Manipulative therapy, is a physical treatment to treat musculoskeletal pain and disability; it most includes kneading and manipulation of muscles.

CUPPING THERAPY

CUPPING THERAPY

Cupping therapy is a form of alternative medicine in which a therapist puts special cups on your skin, which helps with pain, inflammation, and many more.

HYDROTHERAPY

HYDROTHERAPY

Hydrotherapy involves special exercises that you do in a warm-water pool, which helps in arthritis and related rheumatic complaints.

CRYOTHERAPY

CRYOTHERAPY

Cryotherapy is the local or general use of low temperatures in medical therapy. Cryotherapy may be used to treat a variety of tissue lesions.

STRENGTHENING EXERCICES

STRENGHENING EXERCICES

Muscle and bone strenghening exercices such as stretching, chin tuck and many more.

INFRARED THERAPY

INFRARED THERAPY

Infrared therapy is a simple and painless therapy that uses light to treat acute or chronic pain.
